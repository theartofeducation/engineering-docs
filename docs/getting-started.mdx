The documentation here outlines the general-purpose, policies, and responsibilities of a member of the Software Engineering Team at the Art of Education University.

If you are a team member, you should find everything you need to  get started with us, as well as, anything and everything you will need to know to be successful.

If you're not a member of our team, this should give you some insight into how we go about our business.
